About Me

I am a new developer in the Denver metro area. I am excited about creating easily accessible technology to help people in all walks of life live more creative, more fulfilling, more abundant lives. I believe all people deserve nothing less! I am passionate about working with other developers of like mind to bring our dreams of a better world to life.


In that vein, my other passions include: writing fiction, reading fiction, traveling the world, hiking, skiing, going on very long treks, taking on physical challenges, meditating, praying, healing-work, all things spiritual, gardening, cooking, feeding people, sharing big ideas, sharing whatever we have, cheering for the underdog, watching the weather come in, dreaming, imagining, studying, learning, learning new languages, intellectual challenges, playing with children, playing with animals, playing the piano, singing, singing in choirs, laughing, and most of all... redemption.
